Haikyu: [The Dumpster Battle]

is a 2024 Japanese animated sports film that serves as a direct sequel to the "Haikyu!!" anime series. Directed and written by Susumu Mitsunaka, the film features an ensemble cast including Ayumu Murase, Kaito Ishikawa, Yuki Kaji, and Yuichi Nakamura. The story centers on the long-anticipated volleyball match between Karasuno High School and Nekoma High School during the Spring Nationals. This encounter, known as "The Dumpster Battle," marks the first official face-off between the two schools, fulfilling a dream they've nurtured for years.

Genre: drama, action, and coming-of-age
